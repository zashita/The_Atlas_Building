ABOUT US

The building

The Atlas building is an architectural icon, that combines the sophistication of the city with contemporary urban style. Inspired by the innovation of tech city and the creative buzz of neighbouring Shoreditch, it’s a building that truly reflects the spirit of its surroundings.

Designed by award-winning Architects MAKE and Woods Bagot, the building’s staggered construction is formed from six architectural fins that are as distinctive as they are elegant. The 40-storey residential tower and nine-storey office building is complemented by public spaces for the whole community.

The company

Rocket Properties is the private development company behind The Atlas Building. Established for over 10 years, it has a proven track record, with experience of many mixed-use development projects in London, including several in and around the City Road and Old Street.

To date, Rocket Properties has developed for its clients, hotels, retail space, and more recently offices and student housing. It identifies potential investment opportunities, taking them through the planning process and beyond.
